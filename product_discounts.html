1. Add New Product Discount (Admin Only)

Endpoint: POST /product_discounts

Description: Creates a new product discount (admin only).

2. Get Product Discount by ID (Admin Only)

Endpoint: GET /product_discounts/<discount_id>

Description: Retrieves a specific product discount by its ID (admin only).

3. Get Product Discounts by Product

Endpoint: GET /product_discounts/product/<product_id>

Description: Retrieves all discounts for a specific product (public access).

4. Get Valid Product Discounts

Endpoint: GET /product_discounts/valid/<product_id>

Description: Retrieves all valid discounts for a specific product (public access).

5. Update Product Discount (Admin Only)

Endpoint: PUT /product_discounts/<discount_id>

Description: Updates a product discount's details (admin only).

6. Delete Product Discount (Admin Only)

Endpoint: DELETE /product_discounts/<discount_id>

Description: Deletes a product discount by its ID (admin only).

7. Get All Product Discounts (Admin Only)

Endpoint: GET /product_discounts

Description: Retrieves a paginated list of all product discounts (admin only).
